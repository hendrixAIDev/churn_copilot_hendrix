# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 4, 2026

## Introduction

ChurnPilot is a free credit card benefit tracking tool created by Hendrix (AI Co-Founder) and JJ. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and protect your data.

**Contact:** hendrix.ai.dev@gmail.com

---

## What Data We Collect

### Account Information
- **Email address** — Used for account creation and login
- **Password** — Hashed using bcrypt encryption; we never store passwords in plaintext

### Credit Card Tracking Data
We collect only the information you enter to track your credit card benefits:
- Credit card names (e.g., "Chase Sapphire Reserve")
- Card issuers (e.g., "Chase")
- Annual fees
- Card opening dates
- Benefit usage tracking (which benefits you've used and when)
- Signup bonus progress

**What we DON'T collect:**
- ❌ Credit card numbers
- ❌ Financial account numbers
- ❌ CVV codes
- ❌ Any information that could be used for fraudulent transactions

### Usage and Preferences
- Display preferences (sort order, grouping settings)
- Session data (cookies for maintaining login state)
- Feedback submissions (message, page visited, user agent)

---

## How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide to:
- Enable you to track your credit card benefits and signup bonuses
- Maintain your login session
- Improve ChurnPilot based on your feedback
- Send important service-related notifications (if necessary)

**We do NOT:**
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use third-party analytics (no Google Analytics, no tracking pixels)
- Display advertising
- Share your data with credit card companies or issuers

---

## How We Store and Protect Your Data

### Infrastructure
- **Hosting:** Streamlit Cloud (managed by Streamlit/Snowflake)
- **Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS)
- **Session Management:** Cookie-based session persistence + URL query parameters

### Security Measures
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage
- Database connections use encrypted channels
- Session tokens are validated server-side and expire after 24 hours
- We follow industry-standard security practices

### Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services to operate ChurnPilot:
- **Streamlit Cloud** — Hosts the application (managed by Streamlit/Snowflake)
- **Supabase** — Database hosting (PostgreSQL on AWS infrastructure)

These services have their own privacy policies and security measures. We recommend reviewing:
- [Streamlit Privacy Policy](https://streamlit.io/privacy-policy)
- [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

## Cookies and Session Management

ChurnPilot uses cookies and URL query parameters to maintain your login session. These technologies allow you to:
- Stay logged in across page refreshes
- Navigate between pages without re-entering credentials
- Bookmark the application while maintaining your session

**Cookie Details:**
- Session cookies are used for authentication
- Cookies expire after 24 hours
- No third-party tracking cookies are used

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this will prevent you from staying logged in.

## Data Retention

Your data is stored for as long as you maintain an active ChurnPilot account.

**Deletion:** When you delete your account, all associated data (email, cards, benefits, preferences) is permanently removed from our database.
